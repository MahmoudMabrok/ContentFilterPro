## Full Description

### Master Your Feed: Take Control of Your Social Media Experience
Content Filter Pro gives you the power to declutter your social media feeds. Say goodbye to distracting posts, irrelevant updates, and unwanted sponsored content on LinkedIn, Facebook, and Reddit. Customize your browsing experience with advanced filtering rules tailored precisely to your needs.

### Key Features:
- **Multi-Platform Support**: Seamlessly works on LinkedIn, Facebook, and Reddit.
- **Advanced Filtering Rules**: Create custom rules based on keywords, author names, connection degrees (LinkedIn), and more.
- **Smart Actions**: Choose whether to completely hide matching posts or highlight them for review.
- **Global Toggles**: Instantly hide all promoted/sponsored content or noisy feed updates with a single click.
- **Multi-Condition Logic**: Combine multiple rules using AND/OR logic for granular control.
- **Privacy First**: Your rules and data are stored locally on your device. We do not track your browsing history or collect personal data.
- **Stats Tracking**: See exactly how much noise you've filtered out with built-in statistics.
- **Premium Design**: A sleek, modern interface with full Dark Mode support.

### Why Content Filter Pro?
Social media algorithms often prioritize engagement over relevance. Content Filter Pro puts you back in the driver's seat, allowing you to focus on the content that actually matters to you. 
- **On LinkedIn**: Filter out recruitment spam, "liked" posts from your network, or specific job titles.
- **On Facebook**: Remove sponsored ads and noisy "suggested" content.
- **On Reddit**: Clean up your feed by filtering specific subreddits or authors.

### How it Works:
1. Open the extension popup toggle to enable/disable filtering globally.
2. Visit the Options page to create sophisticated filtering rules.
3. Use the Statistics tab to see your impact.
4. Enjoy a cleaner, more focused social media experience!

### Permissions & Privacy:
- **Storage**: To save your custom filtering rules and settings locally.
- **Tabs**: To identify the current site and show relevant statistics in the popup.
- **Privacy Policy**: We take your privacy seriously. All filtering logic and data remain strictly on your local device. Read our full [Privacy Policy](file:///Users/mahmoud.maghrabia/Documents/Extensions/MyRightHand/PRIVACY_POLICY.md) for more details.

- **storage**: Required to store user-defined filtering rules, general settings, and local filtering statistics.
- **activeTab**: Used to allow the extension to securely interact with the current tab when the popup is opened.
- **tabs**: Required to read the URL of the current tab to display site-specific filtering status and statistics in the popup.

- **Host Permissions (e.g., *://*.linkedin.com/*, *://*.facebook.com/*, *://*.reddit.com/*)**: Required to allow the extension to inject filtering logic and analyze content specifically on these supported platforms. Statically declaring these hosts ensures the extension only operates on the intended sites, adhering to the principle of least privilege while providing full filtering functionality.

- **Single Purpose**: The extension has a single, well-defined purpose: to provide users with advanced content filtering capabilities on social media platforms (LinkedIn, Facebook, Reddit) to improve their browsing experience and reduce noise.
